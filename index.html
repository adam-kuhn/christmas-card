Kia ora ("Hello in Maori")! We hope that everyone is having a lovely Christmas surrounded by friends and family.

This is our first true Christmas together as we are both away from our family and friends, so now we can start to develop our own traditions. Our Christmas present to ourselves is a weekend trip to Waiheke Island, where we will do a wine tour, lay on the beach, swim in the ocean, and explore the island on bikes. We will return from Waiheke on Christmas Eve. On Christmas Day the plan is to have a relaxing morning, have coffee (and Bailey's), and make banana chocolate chip pancakes. The once again it'll be off to the beach where we will lay in the sun and have a picnic for lunch. For dinner we plan to make quite the feast! For starters we will have cheese and crackers (with wine) and, smoked salmon and rye bread. Then it'll be beetroot casserole, mashed potatoes, spinach salad, and ham. And if we still aren't full there is even a potential for some cookies for dessert.

Adam has enjoyed his program so far and is quite exciting to begin studying the classroom to learn even more.
